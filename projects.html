HR Management Optimization

HR Management Optimization is a project to improve the hiring process using BPMN 2.0 and transformational frameworks. By analyzing the current workflow, the project removed unnecessary steps to speed up screening.

The new process reduces "time to hire" from 21 to 14 days and improves interview efficiency. It helps HR teams hire faster, save time, and cut costs - all with the same resources.
